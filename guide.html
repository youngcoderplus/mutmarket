We Sell Both New And Second-Hand Products

This website is a student marketplace where you can buy and sell second-hand or newitems like books, electronics, and clothing.

How to Use the Student Marketplace

For Buyers:

  1. Visit the website and browse the available products.
  2. For each item, choose the quantity you want from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click "Add to Cart" to add it to your cart.
  4. To view or manage your cart, click the cart icon at the top right of the page.
  5. You can remove items from the cart if you change your mind.
  6. Check the total price for all items in your cart.
  7. Click "Place Your Order" to be redirected to Alex Mwangi's WhatsApp with your order details.
  8. Send the message on WhatsApp. Alex will confirm the items and help you communicate with the seller.

For Sellers:

  1. Click "Sell Product" in the navigation menu.
  2. Fill in the item name, price, and description.
  3. Do not upload images here. Images are sent later via WhatsApp to Alex.
  4. Click "Submit" to be redirected to Alex Mwangi's WhatsApp.
  5. Send the product image via WhatsApp and discuss the listing with Alex.
  6. Once approved, your item will appear on the marketplace for buyers.

Note: Always follow the Terms & Conditions. Alex Mwangi only facilitates communication to make buying and selling safe and simple.
